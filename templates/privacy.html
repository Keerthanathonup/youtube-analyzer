1. Introduction

Welcome to YInsights ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our YInsights platform.

2. YouTube API Services

YInsights uses the YouTube API Services to provide you with content analysis capabilities. By using our service, you are also agreeing to:

  • Google's Privacy Policy
  • YouTube's Terms of Service

Our use of YouTube API Services adheres to YouTube API Services Terms of Service.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 YouTube Data

Through the YouTube API Services, we temporarily access and process:

  • Video metadata (titles, descriptions, channel names, etc.)
  • Video transcripts/captions
  • Thumbnail images

We do not download, store, or retain actual video or audio content from YouTube.

3.2 Data Retention

We temporarily store YouTube data for a maximum of 5 days, which is well within YouTube API Services terms (which allow up to 30 days). Analysis results derived from this data may be retained longer as they represent our service's value-added output.

3.3 Revoking Access

You can revoke YInsights' access to your Google account data at any time through Google Security Settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through YouTube API Services to:

  • Generate AI-powered analysis of video content, including summaries, key points, topic identification, and sentiment analysis
  • Create relationship networks between related videos
  • Display video thumbnails and metadata in our interface
  • Improve our services and develop new features

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

Our service incorporates and depends on several third-party services:

  • YouTube API Services (Google LLC)
  • Claude API (Anthropic)

Each of these services has its own Privacy Policy, and your use of YInsights is subject to these policies as well.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
